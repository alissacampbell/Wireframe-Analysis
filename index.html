Style observations

  • Text: Thin Arial that gives a clean and modern look to the content. Mix of very small and large weight. Is aligned pretty much center for the entire page. No italics
  • palette: Cool greys, blue, and brown
  • Images: Section one, image takes up entire section and text is places on top of image. Section two, image is on the right side and text was on the left. Section four, had three images consisting of one large photo on the left and two small photos on the right
  • Header: Centered at the top of the page
